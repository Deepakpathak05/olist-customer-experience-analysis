# Understanding Customer Experience & Retention Drivers in Brazilian E-commerce

## A Data-Driven Analysis Using the Olist E-commerce Dataset

This project presents a business-focused exploratory data analysis (EDA) and statistical analysis of the Olist Brazilian E-commerce dataset. The analysis explores customer behavior, delivery performance, customer satisfaction, spending patterns, and retention trends to uncover actionable business insights.

---

# Project Objective

The primary objective of this project is to analyze operational and customer-related factors influencing:

- Customer satisfaction
- Revenue generation
- Delivery performance
- Customer retention
- Spending behavior

The project also uses statistical hypothesis testing to validate business assumptions using data-driven analysis.

---

# Dataset Used

This project uses the publicly available Olist Brazilian E-commerce dataset containing information related to:

- Customers
- Orders
- Payments
- Products
- Sellers
- Reviews
- Delivery timelines
- Geolocation data

# Statistical Hypothesis Testing

## Hypothesis 1
### Late Deliveries Reduce Customer Satisfaction

Result:
- Statistical testing confirmed that delayed deliveries significantly reduce customer review scores.

Business Insight:
- Delivery performance emerged as one of the strongest operational drivers of customer satisfaction.

---

## Hypothesis 2
### Repeat Customers Spend More Than First-Time Customers

Result:
- Repeat customers spent significantly more compared to first-time customers.

Business Insight:
- Repeat customers generated more than 2× higher average spending, highlighting the importance of customer retention strategies.

---

# Key Business Insights

- Delayed deliveries strongly impact customer satisfaction.
- Repeat customers contribute significantly higher revenue.
- Revenue is concentrated among a small number of product categories.
- Customer retention and logistics optimization represent major business growth opportunities.

---

# Business Recommendations

- Improve logistics efficiency to reduce delivery delays.
- Invest in customer retention and loyalty programs.
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing product categories.
- Monitor seller performance to improve customer experience.
